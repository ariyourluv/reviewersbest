Smart bulbs? Absolutely DIY-friendly. LED strips? Usually straightforward. Smart switches and dimmers, especially in older homes? Unless you're comfortable with electrical work, bring in a pro. Planning Your System

Before buying anything, map out your home's lighting zones. Consider:

  • Which areas need color capability versus white spectrum only
  • Where you want manual switches to remain functional
  • Which lighting you'll control primarily via voice, app, or automation
  • Your budget for initial installation versus room-by-room expansion

I started with main living areas and bedroom, then expanded to hallways and bathrooms. This phased approach spread the investment and let me learn what worked before committing fully.
