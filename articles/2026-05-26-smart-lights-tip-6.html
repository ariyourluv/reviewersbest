Common Mistakes to Avoid

Using Cheap Hubs and Bulbs: The $15 smart bulbs that constantly drop connection will drive you insane. Stick with established brands like Philips Hue, LIFX, or Lutron.

Ignoring Color Rendering Index (CRI): Anything below CRI 90 will make your expensive furnishings and artwork look dull. Premium bulbs offer CRI 95+, which renders colors as accurately as natural sunlight.

Over-Automating: Not everything needs to be automatic. I keep manual override capabilities for all my scenes because sometimes you just want specific lighting, regardless of what time it is.

Forgetting About Guests: Create a "Guest Mode" that simplifies your system. Your visitors shouldn't need a tutorial to turn on the bathroom light.
